Hi, I am Avinash Kumar Singh, if you are reading this, I would thank you for visiting my page. Well, I start my introduction with my current postal address. I live in Umea, a city of Northern Sweden. It’s a small city but a gorgeous one. For me, it looks like a set of the film, the buildings the roads and sometime peoples. To me, Swedish people are so humble, kind and helpful and yes, they love potatoes. I work in Umea University as a postdoctoral research in the computer science department. My work is to let robots understand their limitation. You didn’t get it, no issue, nobody gets it in first time. I work in human robot interaction where I am trying to train pepper humanoid robot to understand the natural language and corelate it with its visual perception. Like if you ask robot to pass apple to you, it should understand the apple in language and corelate the apple that it sees. When its not able to establish the correlation, it asks human to help by giving more information either in language or in visual prospect. If you want to hear more about this, please click me.
